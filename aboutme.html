A little bit about me...

I was born and raised in southern California, but decided to move out to the East Coast to attend Columbia University, where I graduated in 2018 with a major in Neuroscience and Behavior. While at Columbia, I had considered medicine as a potential career choice and decided to take a full-time Clinical Research Assistant position at Boston Children’s Hospital (BCH) upon graduating, where I continue to work to this day. While I love the research I’ve been able to do at BCH, I began to further doubt that medicine was truly the career path for me.

Come the fall of 2020, I was actively considering a career change when a friend suggested I try out coding – at least take one course, he said. I did just that and was hooked. I’m still working full-time, but I spend my nights and weekends studying or building things that interest me.
